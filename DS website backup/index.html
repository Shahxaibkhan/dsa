Welcome to Development Strategies

Development Strategies endeavors to improve the human development indicators through systems strengthening and enhancing equitable access to opportunities and social sector services particularly for most deserving and neglected population segments. Development Strategies specializes in adding value to organizational potential through strategic planning, research and analysis, program designing, needs assessments, training and capacity building, setting up management, information, monitoring and supervisory systems, implementation and management of projects, and conducting assessments and evaluations. Development Strategies works with communities and community based organizations, public and private sector organizations, non-government entities, UN agencies, and national and international donors. read more...
