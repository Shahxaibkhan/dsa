Our Values Who we are What we do Why us

Development Strategies is established with the mission to contribute towards improving health, education and quality of life of most deserving people.

We are committed to working in partnerships to strengthen policies and systems leading to achievement of the MGDs and SGDs.

Our prime focus remains on enhancing opportunities and access towards quality services for most deserving segments of population through systems' strengthening and restructuring at all levels.

The following values inspire our work:

  • We are a people centered business entity
  • We are pro-active, learning organization and we invest our surplus in new technologies, people and products that further our mission
  • We are committed to an evidence-based approach
  • We place a high value on our partnerships with organizations, clients, fellow professionals and communities we work with
  • We believe that people have potential, are willing to change and only they can develop themselves.

Development Strategies is established by professionals with extensive experience in health, education and development sector. We are a team of professionals with motivation to facilitate people centered development efforts of public and private sector, targeted at improving health, education and reducing poverty specifically for the most deserving segments of the population.

We have extensive experience of successfully undertaking strategic planning, designing, implementing and evaluating health, development and other social sector programs while working at senior positions with the governmental, semi government, non-governmental and donor organizations.

We are well conversant with organizational structures, operational dynamics, administrative policies and procedures of the government and non-government organizations.

Development Strategies assists organizations in improving social sector indicators and reducing poverty through strengthening and restructuring the systems, enhancing equitable access to opportunities and services, and reducing inequalities.

We assist and facilitates organizations in public and private sectors in achieving their goals and objectives through providing innovative solutions.

We add value to organizational potential through strategic planning, research and analysis; conducting need assessments, training and capacity building; designing programs, setting up management, information, monitoring and supervisory systems; implementing and managing programs; and conducting assessments and evaluations.

We work with public and private sector organizations; non-government entities; UN agencies; and national and international donors.

Development Strategies ensures evidence-based, realistic and quality support to our clients. We help reaching our clients to insights that are convincing and practically do-able on the ground.

Our professionals mantain excellent records of accomplishments in delivering quality work.

We believe in partnerships to strengthen policies and systems that lead to achievement of the MGDs and SGDs.

Our prime focus remains on enhancing access to opportunities, and quality of services for most deserving segments through systems' strengthening at all levels.
