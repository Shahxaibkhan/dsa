News & Announcements

Stay up to date with our latest project awards, team achievements, and development sector insights.

Feb
2017

Advisory Assistance to the Ministry of National Health Services Completed

Dr. Ahsan Maqbool Ahmad, Director Strategies and Programs at Development Strategies, has successfully completed providing advisory assistance to the Ministry of National Health Services, Regulations and Coordination (MNHSRC), supported by John Snow Inc. (JSI). The advisory engagement focused on strengthening health policy and programme design at federal level.

Jan
2017

DS Leads Final Evaluation of EU-Funded WINS Programme in Sindh

Dr. Moazzam Khalil, Founder and CEO of Development Strategies, is part of the team conducting the Final Evaluation of the European Union's Women and Children Improved Nutrition in Sindh (WINS) Programme and Nutrition Profiling of Ten Districts of the Province of Sindh, Pakistan. The EU-funded WINS programme targeted improved nutrition outcomes for women and children under five through an integrated approach.

Jan
2017

Development Strategies Assisting Swabi District Health Programme

Dr. Muhammad Khalid, Director Operations at Development Strategies, is assisting the local government of Swabi District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, in developing its Health Programme, with technical and financial support from Palladium International. The engagement focuses on developing a district-level health strategy with a strong primary healthcare systems component.

Dec
2016

WHO Awards National LHW EPI Evaluation Contract to Development Strategies

The World Health Organization has awarded a contract to Development Strategies to conduct the Evaluation of Involvement of Lady Health Workers (LHWs) in Routine EPI Across Pakistan. The evaluation covers 38,900 LHWs who received EPI training across 72 districts in AJK, Balochistan, KPK, Punjab, and Sindh — assessing their practical involvement in routine immunisation activities using a comprehensive mixed-methods approach.
