Our Team

Seasoned professionals with decades of combined experience in public health, social development, and organisational management.

Leadership

Management Team

Our senior leadership brings together expertise across medicine, public health, management, and development strategy.

Dr. Moazzam Khalil
Founder & Chief Executive Officer
MBBS, MPH

A physician by training with an MPH, Dr. Moazzam Khalil presents over 25 years of progressive community health and social development accomplishments. He brings creative leadership, programme design and implementation, systems restructuring, technical assistance to provincial health departments, strategic planning, management, and monitoring and evaluation expertise.

He has solid community health and development insight with the ability to analyse situations, conceptualise and plan new interventions, and streamline operations. With a keen eye for details, he moves effortlessly across all levels of management and stakeholders.

✉️ moazzam@ds.org.pk
Dr. Ahsan Maqbool Ahmad
Director, Strategies & Programs
MBBS, MSc, PhD

A medical graduate with MSc in Epidemiology and Biostatistics from the Aga Khan University and PhD (Health and Community Services) from the University of Melbourne, Australia. He has worked extensively in project development and management for research, facility-based, and community-based healthcare intervention programmes.

His research and consulting areas include infectious diseases (TB, MDR-TB, Avian Flu, disease surveillance), reproductive and maternal and child health, and healthcare systems — covering services utilisation, primary health care, and policy evaluation at district, provincial, and federal levels.

✉️ ahsan@ds.org.pk
Zulfiqar Haider
Director, Administration & Finance
MBA

With 34 years of service with the Pakistan Air Force — 30 in uniform followed by four years as a Human Resource consultant — Zulfiqar Haider brings unparalleled experience in personnel policy formulation, organisational restructuring, and administration management.

He served at the senior human resource appointment (Assistant Chief of Air Staff – Personnel), where he formulated personnel policies for staffing, recruitment, career progression, and discipline. He subsequently served as Chairman Establishment Review Team, right-sizing the PAF establishment. He later contributed to improving school children's health with the National Commission for Human Development.

✉️ zulfiqar@ds.org.pk
Dr. Muhammad Khalid
Director, Operations
MBBS, MPH (with Distinction, University of Warwick)

A medical doctor with a Masters in Public Health with distinction from the University of Warwick, UK (2007). He brings more than a decade's experience working with government and private sector, including JICA, GIZ, DFID, UN agencies, and international and national NGOs.

His prime areas of research include Health Economics (Economic Evaluations, Health Accounting), Maternal and Child Health (family planning, reproductive health, community case management), Health Systems (services utilisation, quality of care), Nutrition (CMAM, School Health), WASH, and Child Rights. He is affiliated with the Royal Society for Public Health (RSPH), IHEA, and the South Asian Public Health Forum.

✉️ khalid@ds.org.pk
