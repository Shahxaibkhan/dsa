Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Human Development

Development Strategies is committed to improving health, education, and quality of life for Pakistan's most deserving communities. We work across the full development cycle, from design to evaluation.

We collaborate with communities, public and private sector organisations, NGOs, UN agencies, and international donors to create sustainable impact at district, provincial, and national levels.

Our Work

Selected Assignments

A snapshot of our work with international and national partners across Pakistan.

2025–26
PPIF

Endline Evaluation: Scale-Up of Innovative Approach to Family Planning in LHW Rural Uncovered Areas

Evaluated the effectiveness of the NOOR Call programme across ten districts of Punjab. Total household sample of 9,654 Married Women of Reproductive Age collected July to August 2025.

📍 10 Districts, Punjab
2025
PPIF

Baseline Study on Scale-Up of Male Engagement in Family Planning Program

Baseline study for the integration of male health workers and service providers in the family planning ecosystem across 3 districts (4 units) of Punjab.

📍 3 Districts, Punjab
2018–2019
Nutrition International

End-of-Project Assessment: Scaling Zinc & ORS for Childhood Diarrhea

Data collected across 15 districts in Punjab and Sindh to assess outcomes of scaled interventions for treating childhood diarrhea with zinc and low osmolality ORS.

📍 Punjab & Sindh (15 Districts)
