About Development Strategies

A people-centred consultancy committed to improving lives through evidence, expertise, and partnership.

Our Mission

Improving Human Development Indicators

Development Strategies was established with a clear mission: to contribute towards improving health, education and quality of life of the most deserving people in Pakistan.

We are committed to working in partnerships to strengthen policies and systems leading to the achievement of the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our prime focus remains on enhancing opportunities and access towards quality services for the most deserving segments of population through systems strengthening and restructuring at all levels.

We specialise in adding value to organisational potential through strategic planning, research and analysis, programme designing, needs assessments, training and capacity building, setting up management information, monitoring and supervisory systems, implementation and management of projects, and conducting assessments and evaluations.

Development Strategies works with communities and community-based organisations, public and private sector organisations, non-government entities, UN agencies, and national and international donors.

Our Identity

Who We Are

Development Strategies is a professional consultancy firm headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan, with a regional office in Sukkur, Sindh. We are a multidisciplinary team of physicians, public health specialists, social scientists, educators, management experts, and development practitioners.

Our senior leadership collectively brings decades of hands-on experience across Pakistan's most challenging development landscapes — from remote rural districts to federal policy corridors. We have worked in all four provinces, Azad Kashmir, Gilgit-Baltistan, and FATA.

We are registered with the relevant regulatory bodies in Pakistan and operate under the highest standards of professional and ethical conduct. Our team has strong affiliations with national and international professional associations including the Royal Society for Public Health (RSPH), the International Health Economics Association (IHEA), and the South Asian Public Health Forum.

Our Services

What We Do

Development Strategies operates across the full development and research cycle, offering end-to-end technical expertise to our clients and partners.

Strategic Planning

Development of strategic plans for government departments, NGOs, and donor agencies, including implementation and monitoring frameworks.

Research & Analysis

Quantitative and qualitative research, surveys, situational analyses, KAP studies, baseline and end-line assessments.

Programme Design

Designing research and development projects, writing proposals for private and public sector, including PC-1s and grant applications.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Setting up M&E and supervisory systems, MIS and database management, and conducting mid-term and end-of-project evaluations.

Organisational Strengthening

Systems analysis and restructuring, organisational audits, management restructuring, and institutional capacity development.

Training & Capacity Building

Developing training modules, conducting trainings for community workers, managers, and government functionaries at all levels.
